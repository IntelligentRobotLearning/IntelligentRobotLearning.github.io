Dr. Shuzhen Luo, PhD, is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Daytona Beach, FL since 2023 Fall. Her work lies in the intersection between human biomechanics, wearable robotics, control and machine learning. Before joining ERAU, she has worked as a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Rutgers University, New Jersey Institute of Technology, NC State University and UNC-Chapel Hill. She got her PhD degree at Nankai University.
