About

Dr. Shuzhen Luo, PhD, is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Daytona Beach, FL since 2023 Fall. Her work lies in the intersection between human-robot interaction, machine learning, wearable robotics, control and biomechanics. Before joining ERAU, she worked as a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Rutgers University, New Jersey Institute of Technology, NC State University and UNC-Chapel Hill. She obtained Ph.D. degree at Nankai University.
